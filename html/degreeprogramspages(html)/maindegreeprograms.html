Degree Programs

Degree programs (i.e. majors) are a vehicle for developing foundational skills and knowledge in a field of your choice. We’ve designed our degrees in ways that provide breadth in key areas while affording students opportunities to explore and pursue depth in particular domains or problem spaces of interest.

All ALU Programs and Degrees are Accredited by the Following Boards and Universities.

3 Paths, All Amazing
Experiences

ALU Offers three main program paths to take during your educational journey with us: The ALU Undergraduate Program, The ALU Postgraduate Program, The ALU Study Abroad Program.

The ALU Undergraduate Program

All degrees are accredited offered between the two ampuses. They are 3-4 years in Length, where 8 months are spent on Campus and 4 Months at Internships every year, resulting in possibly gaining up to 1 years worth of work experience.

You will spend your first year in ALU’s unique ‘Leadership Core’ which focuses on developing key ‘21st Century’ skills through courses such as Data and Decisions, Real World Projects, Communicating for Impact and Entrepreneurial Leadership. From year two onward, you enter a more specialised degree path of your choice. Degree programs in Mauritius are awarded by our founding academic partner, Glasgow Caledonian University, where students will receive this internationally recognised UK-accredited degree. Degree programs in Rwanda are accredited by the Higher Education Council of Rwanda and awarded by the African Leadership University.

The ALU Postgraduate Program

The ALU MBA is a first-of-a-kind offering in Africa—and indeed in the world. It is designed to radically shift your trajectory as a rising executive. Through this unique 20-month, part-time program, you will enhance your skills as a leader, develop a powerful network across Africa and deepen your expertise in business. To view more porgramme details and information on specific offered degreess visit the ALU MBA website.

ALU Study Abroad

The ALU Study Abroad Program is for International students looking to immerse themselves in a life-changing cultural experience, meet young people from diverse backgrounds and develop skills they can use in University or in their first job. Study Abroad students participate in ALU’s Leadership Core and have the optional extra to participate in an internship with one of our Employer Partners on the African continent. Students can select to participate from as short as 4 months to a full year. To view more porgramme details and information on specific offered degreess visit the ALU Study Abroad website.

About ALU

ALU provides higher education for a higher purpose. Our students declare missions, not majors. They develop the real-world skills to take on the world’s most pressing challenges. And they take ownership of their learning from day one through our peer and student-led approach – because ALU believes in the power and agency of young people to start shaping the future right now. Together with a world-class faculty and staff, our students are igniting a ripple of positive impact across Africa and the world. Join us
